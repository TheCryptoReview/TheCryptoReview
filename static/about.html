About

Meet the developers behind the graphs, datapoints, and models that created The Crypto Review

Who we are?

In today's online world, gone are the wall street days when people traded assets in person; but the future has arrvied in the form of blockchain. To meet these demands, we created The Crypto Review, a free cryptocurrency visualizer that predictors, graphs, and reports currency information. Through powerful tools like machine learning, data visualization, and web-scraping, The Crypto Review provides insight into the future of the crypto market.

Our project boastes our hand-designed crypto-currency dashboard; and you can take a look at market trends, predictions, and today's relevant & up to date information. The Crypto Review's incredible resources where created through modern concepts including machine learning, data visualization, blockchain APIs, and web-scraping. Some of the technical langauges used include HTML, CSS, SCSS, Javascript, Python, Java, Bootsrap, Json, and other API's.
