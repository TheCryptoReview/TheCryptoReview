Frequently Asked Questions

What is Cryptocurrency?

A cryptocurrency is a form of payment that can circulate without the need for a central monetary authority such as a government or bank. Instead, cryptocurrencies are created using cryptographic techniques that enable people to buy, sell or trade them securely.

What factors affect the price of crypto?

The price of a single cryptocurrency is determined by several factors, including supply and demand, competition, and its regulation. News developments and real world affects such as world events and natural disasters also influence investor perception about blockchain and it's monetary worth.

How and where can I trade crypto safely?

Our website The Crypto Review educates users on the importance of cryptocurrency as a financial system and also as a means to trade static/assets online. Furthermore, it is important to find a safe place to trade cryptocurrency. Popular apps include Coinbase, Gemini, SoFi, and Robinhood.

Why is crypto extremly volatile?

A simple answer could be because it is still at a very nascent stage compared to other forms of investment tools and currency. The result of this newness is high volatility in the industry. In a bid to build wealth quickly, investors are attempting to experiment with their money and also to figure out how cryptocurrency prices fluctuate or whether they could influence its prices.
